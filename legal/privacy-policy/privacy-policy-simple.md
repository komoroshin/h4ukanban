# Healthy4U Privacy Policy (Simple Version)

**Effective Date**: January 27, 2026

## Quick Summary

**We collect health data to provide our service. We protect it with standard security. We don't sell it. You can delete it anytime.**

**Read below for details.**

---

## 1. Who We Are

**Healthy4u Medical Technologies LTD** is an AI-powered health information platform based in Israel.

**Contact**: privacy@healthy4u.world

**What we do**: We provide tools to track health data, store medical records, and get general health information from AI.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Personal Information:
- Name, email, phone number, date of birth
- Account password (encrypted)

### Health Data:
- Symptoms, medical history, diagnoses, medications
- Vitals (heart rate, blood pressure, weight, etc.)
- Lab results and medical documents you upload
- Data from health apps and devices - if you connect them
- Voice recordings (if you use voice features)

### Usage Data (Automatically Collected):
- How you use the app (pages visited, features used)
- Device info (phone type, operating system)
- IP address, location (city/country level)
- AI prompts and responses

### Payment Data:
- Processed by Stripe/PayPal (we don't store full credit card numbers)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### To Provide the Service:
- Store your health records
- Run AI to answer health questions
- Sync data across your devices
- Send you notifications and reminders

### To Improve Our Service:
- Analyze usage patterns (what features people use)
- Improve AI accuracy with **de-identified data** (no personal info)
- Fix bugs and improve performance

### To Communicate:
- Send account updates and security alerts
- Send marketing emails (you can opt out)

### Legal Requirements:
- Comply with laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests (subpoenas, court orders)

---

## 4. AI and Your Data

### Third-Party AI Providers:
- **Your prompts are sent to them** to generate responses
- **They do NOT retain your data** (zero retention policy per contracts)
- **They do NOT train their models on your data**

### Our AI Improvement:
- We may use **de-identified data** (with personal info removed) to improve our AI
- **Opt-in required**: You must enable "Use my data for AI improvement" in Settings
- **Default: OFF** - We don't use your data unless you opt in

---

## 5. Who We Share Data With

### Service Providers (Subprocessors):
- **Cloud hosting**: Cloud infrastructure providers (to store your data)
- **AI providers**: AI service providers (to run AI features)
- **Payment**: Payment processors (to process payments)
- **Analytics**: Analytics providers (to understand app usage - de-identified)

**All service providers sign data protection agreements with us.**

### We Do NOT:
- ✗ Sell your personal health data
- ✗ Share identifiable data with advertisers
- ✗ Give data to third parties for their own purposes

### When We Must Share:
- **Legal requirements**: Court orders, subpoenas, government requests
- **Emergencies**: To prevent harm (e.g., suicide prevention)
- **Business transfer**: If we're acquired, data may transfer to new owner (we'll notify you)

---

## 6. Your Rights

### You Can:

**Access**: Request a copy of your data
- Email privacy@healthy4u.world or use account settings

**Delete**: Request deletion of your account and data
- Takes effect in 30 days; backups deleted in 90 days

**Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- Use account settings or email us

**Export**: Download your data in common formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)
- Always free (no limits)

**Opt-Out**: Stop AI training data use
- Settings → Privacy → Toggle off "Use my data for AI improvement"

**Unsubscribe**: Stop marketing emails
- Click "Unsubscribe" in any email

### Response Time:
- We'll respond within **30 days** (or as required by law)

## 7. Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data:
- ✓ Encryption (data encrypted in storage and transmission)
- ✓ Access controls (only authorized staff can access data)
- ✓ Regular security audits
- ✓ Two-factor authentication (available for all accounts)

### But Remember:
- **No system is 100% secure**
- **You use the service at your own risk**
- Keep your password secure (we're not liable if you lose it)

### If There's a Breach:
- We'll notify you within the time required by law
- GDPR: 72 hours (for EU users)
- CCPA: Without unreasonable delay (for California users)

---

## 8. Data Retention

### While Your Account is Active:
- We keep your data indefinitely (until you delete it or close your account)

### After You Close Your Account:
- **30 days**: You can still access and export data
- **90 days**: Data permanently deleted from our systems
- **12 months**: Encrypted backups automatically expire

### Exceptions:
- Legal requirements may require longer retention (e.g., tax records for 7 years)
- De-identified research data (can't be traced back to you) may be kept indefinitely

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

### Age Requirements:
- **Under 18**: Cannot use Healthy4U
- **18+**: Can use independently

**Why 18+?** Health data is sensitive information. To protect minors and ensure compliance with privacy laws, we require all users to be adults (18 years or older).

**If you're under 18**: Please ask a parent or guardian to create an account and manage your health information on your behalf.

## 10. Cookies

### What Are Cookies?
Small files stored on your device when you visit our website.

### What We Use:
- **Essential cookies**: Required for the site to work (login, security)
- **Analytics cookies**: Google Analytics (to see how people use the site - de-identified)
- **Optional cookies**: Marketing, advertising (you can reject these)

### How to Control:
- **Cookie banner**: Accept or reject when you first visit
- **Cookie settings**: Click "Cookie Settings" in footer
- **Browser settings**: Block all cookies (may break some features)

## 12. Compliance

### We Follow These Laws:

**GDPR** (EU): General Data Protection Regulation
- You have rights to access, delete, correct, export data
- Legal basis: Your consent + contractual necessity

**HIPAA** (US): Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
- **Note**: Healthy4U is generally NOT covered by HIPAA as we are not a healthcare provider
- However, if US healthcare providers use our platform to store patient data, we can enter into Business Associate Agreements upon request

**CCPA** (California): California Consumer Privacy Act
- California residents have additional rights
- We don't sell personal data

**Israeli Privacy Law**: Privacy Protection Law (Amendment 13, 2025)
- Enhanced protections for medical data
- Data Protection Officer appointed
